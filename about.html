关于李琪 About Liki

李琪 Liki，男，艺术家。创作涉及行为艺术、社会性艺术、装置、表演与行为影像。他持续从身体经验、公共空间、亲密关系与劳动结构中寻找可被重新感知的现场。

Liki is a male artist working with performance art, social art, installation, live performance, and moving image. His work searches for sites that can be perceived again through bodily experience, public space, intimacy, and structures of labor.
