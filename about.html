李琪Li QiLiki

李琪，2000 年出生于陕西宝鸡。2024 年本科毕业于西安美术学院跨媒体艺术系实验艺术专业，现为同院 2024 级实验艺术专业硕士研究生。

他的研究聚焦身体行为与社会参与性艺术，持续探索身体与空间、个体与社会之间的动态关系。创作多以行为影像、互动装置和社会参与式艺术为媒介，从“小动作中的大哲学”切入现实语境。

李琪的实践常建立在田野调查与现场实验之上，重视与观众、参与者以及特定空间的互动；作品既是个人表达，也是一种让现场思维彼此碰撞的方式。

Li Qi, born in Baoji, Shaanxi in 2000, graduated from Experimental Art at the Department of Transmedia Art, Xi'an Academy of Fine Arts in 2024. He is currently a master's student in Experimental Art at the same academy.

His research focuses on bodily action and socially engaged art, exploring the dynamic relations between body and space, individual and society. His practice uses performance video, interactive installation, and participatory art.

Built through fieldwork and site experiments, his works value encounters with viewers, participants, and particular places. They are both personal expressions and spaces where situated thought can collide.
