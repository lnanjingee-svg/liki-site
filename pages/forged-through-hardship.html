作品文字 / 展开阅读Work text / Read note

在《千锤百炼》中，艺术家以不断敲击一枚倒置钉子的动作，隐喻现代社会中个体在无形压力下的自我消耗与身份解构。每一次木锤的挥动，不仅侵蚀着工具本身，也象征着个体在追寻社会认同与价值过程中逐步瓦解的精神状态。

随着锤子最终破碎，行为宣告结束，揭示了在持续的社会驱动与文化神话建构中，个体力量的耗尽与自我意识的崩塌。

In Forged Through Hardship, the artist repeatedly strikes an inverted nail fixed to the ground with a wooden hammer. This action reflects self-consumption and identity deconstruction under invisible social pressure.

As the hammer finally breaks, the performance ends, revealing the exhaustion of individual strength and the collapse of self-awareness amid social drives and cultural mythmaking.

重复并不制造结果，它先让工具、身体和意志同时磨损。Repetition does not produce a result first. It wears down tool, body, and will together.
