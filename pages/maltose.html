《麦芽糖》围绕粘连、拉伸与消耗展开。甜味不再只是味觉经验，而被转化为一种黏附在身体、动作和时间上的状态。

Maltose unfolds around stickiness, stretching, and exhaustion. Sweetness is no longer only taste; it becomes a condition attached to body, action, and time.

被拉长的不是材料本身，而是身体停留在一个动作里的时间。What stretches is not material alone, but the time a body remains inside an action.
